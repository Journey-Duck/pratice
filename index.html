About Genshin Impact

Genshin is an RPG fantasy open world game. You will be roleplaying a character called Traveler. The Traveler goes on this journey trying to find his lost sibling in Teyvat with their accompanied guide, Paimon. As the Traveler across the nations of Teyvat, they will meet new accompanies and learn the history behind this world.

Story Quest

Archon Quest follows the Traveler finding his lost sibling throughout the different nation of Teyvat. While searching for their sibling they might stumble on a side quest from villagers or to help a different nationâ€™s problems. Friends

In this open word you can invite your friends to explore and help defeat monsters when you are in trouble. Do not have friends to play with but still want to interact with people, well there are mini games that can put you against or work as a team to earn rewards.

Puzzles

While exploring the nation you will come across puzzles to progress through the story or chest to earn rewards. There are diverse types of puzzles like digging up a chest or defeating a monster at a certain time. These puzzles are everywhere, so make sure to look at even the highest places when looking for puzzles and chest.
